Welcome to Sh@reNet












Our Mission


Sh@renet creates a network between various organizations to share resources. It is an e-platform which provides space for uploading the inventory of equipment or items one has and, at the same time, request for the equipment and items one needs. The institutions and organizations can register on this platform with the common intention of multiplying resources by way of sharing. The mission of Sh@renet is to enable sharing and pooling of resources among, and between, various institutions and organizations like health, education, and public works department etc. for the better health, education, wellbeing and common good of the society. This will help the economically challenged African countries to optimally utilize the resources and pursue the path of growth.






How can you help?



Join the Sh@reNet community by making an account and add all your available resources. Anyone who needs that item will contact you via mail and you can discuss the details with them.

Our service is perfect for NGO's to locate what is required and where it is required. They can coordinate with various organizations who are a part of this community and coordinate the sharing and distribution of resouces already available




How to proceed?



  • Click on login
  • Choose the register button and make an account
  • Add all available items and required items
  • Explore the community by searching organization and items!
